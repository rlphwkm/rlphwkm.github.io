About Us

Who We Are

At rlphwkm, we are committed to delivering cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions tailored to businesses of all sizes. Founded on the principles of Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability, our firm ensures that organizations can operate securely in an increasingly digital world.

With a strong foundation in cybersecurity, OSINT, and public safety, rlphwkm provides expert consultation and security solutions that help protect against modern threats.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower businesses by fortifying their security posture. We believe that strong cybersecurity should be accessible, reliable, and effective—not just for large corporations but for small and mid-sized businesses as well.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise & Experience – With a background in cybersecurity, IT, and intelligence, our team brings a wealth of knowledge to every engagement.
  • Tailored Solutions – We understand that every business has unique security needs. Our approach is customized to provide the best protection for your specific industry.
  • Commitment to Security – We prioritize confidentiality, data integrity, and system availability, ensuring that security is never an afterthought.

Our Core Values

  • Confidentiality – Your data is safe with us. We uphold the highest standards of discretion and security.
  • Integrity – Ethical practices guide our work, ensuring trust and transparency in every service we provide.
  • Availability – Security solutions should be reliable and accessible when needed most. We ensure that your systems remain resilient against threats.

Meet the Founder

Ralph Wakim – With a Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity from Marist College and extensive experience in IT, security, and intelligence, Ralph founded rlphwkm to bridge the gap between security awareness and actionable protection. His dedication to cybersecurity and public safety drives the firm's mission to offer top-tier solutions to businesses that need them most.
